Summer
Internships

Our Student Airborne Research Program is an eight-week summer internship for
senior undergraduate students in the United States. Interns assist with NASA's
Airborne Science program, which takes flight to study the Earth.The application
deadline is January 31.

Our Changing
Planet

For more than 50 years, NASA satellites have provided data on Earth's land,
water, air, temperature, and climate. NASA's Earth Information Center
allows visitors to see how our planet is changing in six key
areas: sea level rise and coastal impacts, health and air quality,
wildfires, greenhouse gases, sustainable energy, and agriculture.
